Available Support Channels
--------------------------

Commvault provides multiple ways to contact customer service. The right option depends on the urgency and complexity of your issue.

Support Portal
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

The primary method for contacting Commvault support is through the support portal. Users can create a support ticket, describe their issue, and track progress in one place.

The support portal allows you to:
  * Submit new support requests
  * Monitor case updates
  * Communicate with support engineers
  * Upload logs and screenshots

This method is especially useful for ongoing or complex technical problems.

Phone Support
-------------

For urgent issues, phone support is often the fastest way to get help. Speaking directly with a support representative allows for real-time troubleshooting and immediate feedback.

Phone support is recommended when:
  * There is a critical system failure
  * Backup or recovery processes are not working
  * Business operations are affected

During the call, the support agent may guide you through troubleshooting steps or escalate the issue if needed.

Email Support
-------------

Email support is suitable for non-urgent queries or when you need to provide detailed information. This method allows you to explain the issue thoroughly and include attachments such as system logs or error messages.

To make email support effective:
  * Clearly describe the problem
  * Include account and product details
  * Attach relevant files
  * Mention any steps already taken

Email responses may take longer than phone support, but they provide a clear written record.
